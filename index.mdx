## Key Applications

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Drug Discovery & Development" icon="pills">
    Organoids enable more predictive preclinical testing, reducing attrition rates and accelerating time-to-market for new therapeutics. The technology addresses a $50B+ annual R&D waste from poor predictivity of animal models.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Personalized Medicine" icon="user-doctor">
    Patient-derived organoids allow testing of treatment responses before administration, enabling precision oncology and personalized therapeutic strategies with projected market opportunity of $450B+ by 2030.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Disease Modeling" icon="disease">
    Organoids recapitulate complex disease pathologies, providing unprecedented insights into mechanisms and novel therapeutic targets across cancer, neurodegenerative disorders, and infectious diseases.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Regenerative Medicine" icon="heart-pulse">
    Organoid-based therapies offer potential cures for organ failure, tissue damage, and degenerative conditions, addressing a $30B+ transplantation and regenerative medicine market.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
